Accident Data Center is a site that aggregates local news stories regarding traffic accidents from around the United States. I take care of the site on a day-to-day basis and implement features requested by the marketing team. Sometimes, this involves writing custom PHP plugins; other times, it involves SCSS work.

Personal injury attorneys rent ad space from Accident Data Center by paying us a monthly fee. The site also acts as a place for friends and relatives of those involved in accidents to gather and discuss what happened.

The site is built using Drupal 7. On average, it gets between 10,000 and 30,000 hits per day.
