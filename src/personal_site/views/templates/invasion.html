Lunar Invasion is a two-player action game for Android. I led a team that designed, developed and built the game from scratch over an eight-month period in 2015.

The game was coded entirely from scratch in Java. It uses OpenGL ES for rendering, and has no other dependencies.

In the game, both players control a movable spaceship. They then battle it out, firing energy balls that bounce off walls. The first player to drain the opponent’s life completely is the winner. Players can choose from five distinct weapons, and collect power-ups from destructible terrain and moving satellites.

Lunar Invasion was released on Google Play in October 2015 and costs $2.99. Those wanting a demonstration of the game can get a free copy by contacting me directly.

There’s also a free demo version.
